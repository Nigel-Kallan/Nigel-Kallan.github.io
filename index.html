ABOUT:

Embracing autism and embarking on a journey of understanding and growth with HELPING HANDS, where we are dedicated to nurturing the unique potential within every individual on the autism spectrum. Through personalized therapy programs and unwavering support, we inspire PROGRESS and foster GROWTH, one milestone at a time.

SERVICES:

ABA/IBI - Evidence-based therapy focusing on behavior modification to teach new skills, reduce challenging behaviors, and promote positive behavior change.

SOCIAL and LIFE SKILLS - Structured activities designed to develop essential socialization and life skills.

NATURAL ENVIRONMENT TEACHING - Integrating therapy into everyday activities and learning opportunities in natural contexts.

PARENT COACHING - Providing caregivers with knowledge and support to reinforce skills at home.

ENROLLMENT:

The First step is Intake & Assessment. Request the intake form through our contact form and our senior therapists will review and set an appointment. The next step is to create a Behaviour Treatment Plan. This plan outlines the specific strategies and interventions that will be implemented to address your child's challenges. Once the plan is in place, we will set up the child's therapy program. The goal is to create a seamless and supportive environment that maximizes the progress and success in therapy.
